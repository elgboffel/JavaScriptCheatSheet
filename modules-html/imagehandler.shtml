dis.module.imagehandler.scss: Her findes lidt hjælpeklasser, men det er ikke nødvendigt for at det virker.

HTML:

<div class="imagehandler" data-src-xs="http://placehold.it/480x270" data-src-sm="http://placehold.it/768x432" data-src-md="http://placehold.it/1280x720" data-src-lg="http://placehold.it/1920x1080" data-alt="Bacon bacon bacon"> <noscript> <!-- Search engines indexes images in NOSCRIPT tags. Remember that (Google) robots ignores attributes in url's, so you should use clean ones --> <img src="http://placehold.it/100x56" alt="Bacon bacon bacon"> </noscript> </div>
